Pancakes

What are Pancakes?

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter. It is a type of batter bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • ½ cup wheat germ
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups whole wheat pastry flour

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix eggs with oil and buttermilk. Stir in baking soda, wheat germ, salt and flour; mix until blended.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides, turning once.
